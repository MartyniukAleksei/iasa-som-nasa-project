About This Project

This platform leverages machine learning to assist astronomers in identifying exoplanets through the transit method. By analyzing subtle dips in stellar brightness, our AI can detect the presence of orbiting planets with high accuracy.

The system was trained on thousands of data points from NASA missions such as Kepler and TESS, K2, allowing it to recognize the characteristic features of planetary transits.
